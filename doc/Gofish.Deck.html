    Gofish v0.0.1 Gofish.Deck View Source

    Provides a Deck context to hold the Card struct. The Deck module performs common operations such as creating a new deck and shuffling the cards.

    Examples 

    iex> Gofish.Deck.create
[%Gofish.Deck.Card{suit: "Spades", value: "Ten"},
%Gofish.Deck.Card{suit: "Diamonds", value: "Six"},
%Gofish.Deck.Card{suit: "Clubs", value: "Queen"},
